## Overview

Designed a low-power wearable integrating synchronized ECG and PCG sensors.

## Key Features

- **Edge AI**: Deployed on-device CNN inference on the MAX78000 MCU for real-time cardiac abnormality detection.
- **Low Power**: Optimized the edge AI architecture for offline capability, ensuring reliability without cloud dependency.
- **Multimodal Sensors**: Integrated ECG and PCG sensors.

## Technologies

- C++
- MAX78000
- Edge AI
